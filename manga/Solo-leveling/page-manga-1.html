SUMMARY:

When inter-dimensional “gates” opened up “dungeons” full of monsters, “hunters” blessed with special abilities were humanity’s only defense. Years later, these hunters make a living by “raiding” these dungeons for loot.

Sung Jinwoo, a young bottom-feeder hunter, is overwhelmed by powerful monsters during a routine raid and finds himself in grave danger. Somehow cheating death, he gains access to exclusive quests and perks.

With each quest and perk, he grows stronger without limits. Liberated from his weaknesses, he is now a player of the system, on the rise and Solo Leveling!
